# Marketing Campaign Performance Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes marketing campaign performance using customer demographics, campaign attributes, engagement metrics, and conversion data. The objective is to identify the factors that influence customer conversions and provide actionable insights for improving marketing effectiveness and return on investment (ROI).

## Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* Excel
* Power Query
* Data Visualization
* Business Analytics

## Dataset

The dataset contains 8,000 customer records and 20 variables related to:

* Customer demographics
* Campaign channels and campaign types
* Advertising platforms
* Website engagement
* Email interactions
* Social media engagement
* Customer loyalty
* Conversion outcomes

## Business Questions Answered

* Which campaign channel has the highest performance?
* Which channel achieves the highest CTR?
* Which campaign type attracts the most website visits?
* Which customer segments convert the most?
* Which gender and age groups show higher conversion rates?
* How does customer loyalty impact conversions?
* Which marketing channels should receive more budget allocation?

## Key Insights

### Campaign Performance

* Referral campaigns delivered the strongest overall performance.
* PPC achieved the highest average CTR.
* Social Media showed the weakest overall performance.

### Customer Demographics

* Female customers generated higher conversion rates than male customers.
* Customers aged 63–66 showed the highest conversion levels.
* Higher-income customers represented the largest customer segment.

### Customer Engagement

* Email engagement remained consistent across income groups.
* Higher loyalty levels were associated with significantly higher conversion rates.
* High-loyalty customers achieved over 90% conversion rates.

## Recommendations

1. Increase investment in Referral and PPC campaigns.
2. Strengthen customer loyalty programs.
3. Improve social media engagement strategies.
4. Focus on high-value customer segments.
5. Continuously monitor CTR, conversions, and ROI for optimization.
